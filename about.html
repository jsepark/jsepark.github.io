About Me

Hi there! My name is Joyce and I'm currently a lab manager + researcher at the Cognition and Natural Behaviour Lab.

Education: BA, University of Waterloo, 2021
Major: Honours Psychology (Research Intensive Specialization)
Minors: Cognitive Science and French
Email: joyce.park@uwaterloo.ca

Broadly speaking, I’m interested in how we learn and interact with information. Currently, my projects are focused on (1) how the availability of external devices (i.e., notetaking) affects our memory and our metacognitive judgments (2) how we make decisions based on our judgements of effort and (3) the role of online lecture video quality on student learning

I am looking for opportunities to further explore research in (meta)cognition and decision-making. Specifically, I want to learn more about the factors that influence our judgements of learning and use my research to help inform and improve the educational system.
